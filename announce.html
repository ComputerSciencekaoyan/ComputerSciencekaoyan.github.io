1. About Us

This is a panel of computer science and technology courses. The course content includes programming basis and design, computer hardware, software and system structure. These courses are compulsory for most students majoring in computer science. The participation of the courses mainly involves four forms of activities : lectures, assignments, experimental assignments, and examinations. Canvas system is a lightweight teaching auxiliary website. You can browse your courses and course related materials in this website. In addition, Canvas is committed to providing students with targeted extracurricular resources for students to better learn the course.
